**Project in progress**


Anonymous AI Therapist Platform is a web-based mental health support platform that allows users to communicate freely without creating an account or sharing personal information. The platform offers both AI-generated therapeutic responses and the option to chat with a real therapist, ensuring accessible and private emotional support.

Users can send messages publicly or privately, enabling open community discussions or one-on-one confidential conversations. The system is designed with privacy-first principles, making mental health support available instantly, anonymously, and without barriers such as login or signup.

Key Features:

100% anonymous access (no login or signup required)

AI-powered therapist responses

Optional real therapist interaction

Public and private messaging

Privacy-focused and user-friendly interface

Accessible mental health support anytime
